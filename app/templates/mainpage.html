Customers

Vendors

A Vendor is an person or business that supplies goods or services. Another term for vendor is supplier. In many situations company presents the vendor with a purchase order stating the goods or services needed, the price, delivery date, and other terms. Connect with Purchasing Managers .

Invoices

Invoice is a time-stamped commercial document that itemizes and records a transaction between a buyer & seller. If goods or services were purchased on credit, the invoice usually specifies the terms of the deal and provides information on the available methods of payment.

Bills

A bill is an invoice that one of your suppliers will give you, and which, sooner or later, you will have to pay.It is a Credit or Debit Bills payable are entered to the accounts payable category of a business's general ledger as a credit.The accounts payable will be decreased with a debit entry.
